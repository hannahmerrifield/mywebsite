About

Music Mania is a website that provides you with great album recommendations. It covers all genres from alternative to heavy metal. It rates every album and will give you a brief description of each album; covering who it is written by, a rating and how many tracks it consists of.

Music Mania has insured that there will definitly be an album you have listened to before on our display home page.

To find out the information about the albums, please click albums on the top right corner of your screen.
